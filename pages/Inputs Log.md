## About
Track everything that goes into your body and environment — food, drinks, supplements, sunshine, exercise, sleep, etc.

---
## Log Format
Each entry should follow this pattern:
`- [[date]] HH:MM | category:: value | notes`
### Categories
- `food` — meals, snacks
- `drink` — water, coffee, juice, alcohol, etc.
- `sunshine` — time outdoors / sun exposure
- `exercise` — workouts, walks, movement
- `sleep` — hours, quality
- `other` — anything else worth tracking
