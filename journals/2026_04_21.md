- [[Recipes]] 🥣 Instant Pot Red Lentil Soup
  Ingredients
  
  1 cup red lentils (rinsed)
  1 yellow onion (chopped)
  2–3 carrots (chopped)
  1–2 peppers (any color, chopped)
  3–4 cups water or broth
  2 tbsp oil (olive oil or whatever you have)
  2–3 cloves garlic (optional but recommended)
  1 tsp salt (adjust later)
  
  Optional spices (use what you have):
  
  1 tsp cumin
  1/2 tsp turmeric
  1/2 tsp paprika or chili flakes
  Black pepper
  Steps
  
  1. Sauté (flavor base)
  
  Turn Instant Pot to Sauté
  Add oil, then onion
  Cook ~3–4 min until soft
  Add garlic + spices → cook 30–60 sec (until fragrant)
  
  2. Add everything else
  
  Toss in carrots, peppers, lentils
  Add 3–4 cups liquid
  Stir and scrape bottom (important to avoid burn warning)
  
  3. Pressure cook
  
  Seal lid
  Cook on High Pressure: 5 minutes
  Let it natural release ~10 minutes, then quick release
  Finish
  Stir well (it’ll thicken a lot)
  Add water if too thick
  Taste → adjust salt, pepper, acid (lemon or vinegar if you have it)
  Texture tip
  Red lentils break down = naturally creamy
  If you want it smoother → blend a bit
  If you want it chunkier → use slightly less water
  Easy upgrades (if you’ve got extras)
  Lemon juice at the end → big flavor boost
  Yogurt or sour cream → makes it richer
  Spinach or greens → toss in after cooking
