- [[Morning Journal]] Always Letting Go
	- "Always", "Letting", "Go"... Three words, when taken separately cover a lot of ground.
		- Definitions:
			- Always:
				- All ways taken in to consideration?
				- Every time, Every event, without fail.
				- Beyond even time and space.
		- Letting:
			- Allowing
			- Removing resistence
			- Giving permission
				- NOTE: These each involve the necessity for 2 definable sources. The Letter (one letting), and the Lettee (the one being let/allowed)
		- Go:
			- Move
			- Activate
			- Proceeding
	- To rephrase: What can be said about maintaining continuous mindfulness of the now. There is no other way to be. Theatre happens. To join the drama, is choice. Before allowing the idea of choice there is nothing. This is truth and to think otherwise is ridiculous.
		- Rewrite:
			- Theatre unfolds.
			  Drama begins.
			  Do you enter?
			  
			  Before the choice,
			  where were you?
			  
			  To think there is more
			  is the first mistake.
			  
			  #Koan
